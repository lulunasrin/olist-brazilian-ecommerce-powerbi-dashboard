# Olist Brazilian E-Commerce Analysis Dashboard

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** built using the **Olist Brazilian E-Commerce Dataset** to analyze sales performance, product trends, customer behavior, reviews, and payment insights. The project leverages **Power Query**, **DAX**, and **Star Schema Data Modeling** to transform raw data into meaningful business insights.

---

## 📌 Project Objective

To analyze Olist's e-commerce operations and answer key business questions related to:

- Sales Performance
- Product Performance
- Customer Behavior
- Customer Satisfaction
- Payment Methods
- Installment Preferences

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Data Modeling (Star Schema)

## 📈 Key Insights

- Sales increased steadily during 2017–2018.
- Bed Bath & Table, Health & Beauty, and Sports & Leisure are top-performing categories.
- Customer satisfaction remained high with an average review score of **4.09/5**.
- Customer demand is concentrated in Southeast Brazil.
- Credit Card is the most preferred payment method.
- Most customers prefer single-payment transactions.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Maintain inventory for high-demand categories.
- Focus marketing efforts in Southeast Brazil.
- Expand promotional campaigns in underrepresented regions.
- Improve low-performing categories through targeted promotions.
- Continue providing high-quality customer service.
- Offer flexible payment options to improve customer experience.
